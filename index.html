About the Cakes

Natalie Rose creates delicious unique works of art. We are 100% custom and can turn any idea into a beautiful cake for all events and occasions. Though we specialize in custom cakes, we also make all types of desserts, including cookies, cupcakes, and more! All of our products are made from all natural ingredients.

*You can taste the difference.*


About Natalie Rose

Opening her own cake studio has always been Natalie Rose's dream. Her first bakery job at age 14 was just the start, and her love for baking only deepened. Post high-school she attended and graduated, with top awards, from The Institute of Culinary Education (ICE), where she honed her skills and realized her true passion — cake decorating. Since then Natalie Rose has created hundreds of custom cakes to high praise.

She turned her dream into reality!
